College Benefits

Under the Post-9/11 GI Bill, eligible individuals can receive up to 36 months of education benefits, which can be used for undergraduate and graduate degree programs, vocational and technical training, on-the-job training, and apprenticeships. The amount of financial assistance provided is based on the length of active-duty service and the type of education or training being pursued. Reach out to get more information.
